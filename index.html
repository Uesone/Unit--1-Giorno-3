Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart crangerries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the toppings:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablesppon balsamic vinegard
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 fresh or frozen carnberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mizer with paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dought ingridients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tthough I sometime knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
